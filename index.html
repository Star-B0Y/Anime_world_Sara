My favourite animes

Attack on Titan

It has one of the best storylines to exist even when compared to oscar winning movies. It is a 0 filler anime with the best cliffhanger episodes that also make you experience the best plot twists.

Haikyuu

It is the debated to be the best sports anime to exist and not only gives you the thrill of volleyball, but also teaches you real life lessons like team spirit, sportsman ship and more.

Deathnote

This supernatural shonen anime focuses on battle of minds rather than biceps and makes you question the decisions made by the antagonists as well as the decisions that you yourself have made.
